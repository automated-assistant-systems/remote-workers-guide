# .chapter-map.md

Chapter Architecture Map

Project: **The Remote Worker’s Survival Guide in the AI Age**
Author: Gary G. Bayes, BABA, MBA

Purpose:
This file defines the **conceptual role of each chapter** in the book.
It helps authors, editors, and AI assistants understand how chapters contribute to the overall narrative.

---

# Core Theme

The central idea of the book:

Human Judgment

* AI Assistance
* Smart Workflows
  = **Leverage**

Every chapter should reinforce this idea.

---

# Reader Journey

The book guides readers through three stages:

1. Understand the shift
2. Apply AI in daily work
3. Build a reusable toolkit
