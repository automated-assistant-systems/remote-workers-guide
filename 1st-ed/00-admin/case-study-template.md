Case Study: The Overloaded Consultant

Background
Sarah is a freelance marketing consultant managing five clients remotely.

Problem
Administrative tasks consumed 40% of her workweek.

Solution
She introduced an AI workflow using:

- Notion AI
- Zapier
- ChatGPT

Workflow

1. Meeting notes auto-summarized
2. Client updates auto-generated
3. Task lists created from transcripts

Result

Administrative time dropped from 16 hours per week to 6.
