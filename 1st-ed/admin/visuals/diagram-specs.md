# Part I — Understanding the AI Era

## Chapter 1 — The New Reality of Remote Work

### Visual 01 — Remote Work Before vs After AI
File:
`visuals/part-01-understanding-the-ai-era/chapter-01-the-new-reality-of-remote-work/visual-01-remote-work-before-vs-after-ai.svg`

Layout: Comparison

Before:
- Office-centered workflow
- Meetings as coordination hub
- Documents as primary output
- Limited automation
- Human-managed administrative load

After:
- Distributed digital workflow
- Multiple communication channels
- Task fragmentation across apps
- AI-assisted drafting and synthesis
- Automation layered into routine work

Interpretation goal:
Show that work did not simply move online. Its structure changed.
