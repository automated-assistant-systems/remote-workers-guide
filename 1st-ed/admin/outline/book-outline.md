# Book Outline
The Remote Worker’s Survival Guide in the AI Age  
Practical Tools, Workflows, and Strategies for Thriving Anywhere

This outline defines the canonical structure of the manuscript.  
It is the reference architecture used during editing and restructuring.

The reader journey follows three stages:

Part I — Understanding the AI Era  
Part II — Applying AI to Daily Work  
Part III — The Remote Worker Toolkit

---

# Front Matter

## Foreword
Historical and motivational framing of remote work and AI.

## Introduction
Defines the problem facing remote professionals and introduces the book’s promise.

## How to Use This Book
Explains the structure of the book and how readers can navigate it for practical learning.

# Part I — Understanding the AI Era

Purpose:  
Introduce the shift in remote work, explain the productivity challenges of distributed work, and build the conceptual foundations required for effective AI-assisted workflows.

---

Role: Orientation

- Evolution of remote work
- Why AI is reshaping knowledge work
- Opportunities and risks for remote professionals

Outcome:
Readers understand why remote work and AI must now be considered together.
