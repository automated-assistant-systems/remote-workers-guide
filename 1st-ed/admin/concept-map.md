# Core Concept Framework

The book is built around the following central model:

Human Judgment  
+ AI Assistance  
+ Smart Workflows  
= Professional Leverage

Each part of the book emphasizes one element of this equation.

Part I — Judgment and understanding  
Part II — Practical AI assistance  
Part III — Workflow systems

# Concept Reuse Guidelines

Editors should follow these rules when revising chapters.

Concepts should be:

Introduced once in their primary chapter.

Later chapters should reference earlier explanations rather than repeating them.

Example:

“As discussed in Chapter 6, effective prompts provide clear context and instructions.”

---

# Concept Integrity Rules

Editors and AI writing tools should not:

- introduce new major concepts outside their designated chapters
- redefine concepts introduced earlier
- move conceptual explanations into application chapters

The goal is to preserve the reader’s learning progression.
