# Collaboration, Planning, and Personal Productivity

Distributed teams rely heavily on digital coordination.

Meetings, chat threads, shared documents, and project boards all contribute to collaboration.

Without structure, communication quickly becomes overwhelming.

AI tools help organize this flow of information.

---

## AI as a Coordination Layer

AI can assist collaboration by:

- summarizing meetings  
- extracting action items  
- prioritizing tasks  
- generating project updates  

Instead of manually tracking every conversation, professionals can rely on AI-generated summaries.

*Figure 11.1 — AI-Enhanced Meeting Workflow*

This workflow illustrates how meeting recordings become transcripts, summaries, and action items automatically. AI reduces administrative work while improving clarity across teams.
