# .bookmap.md

Book Architecture Map

Project: **The Remote Worker’s Survival Guide in the AI Age**
Author: Gary G. Bayes, BABA, MBA
Status: Active Manuscript (First Edition)

Purpose:
This file provides a **high-level map of the book’s architecture** so that humans, editors, and AI tools can quickly understand the structure of the manuscript without scanning the entire repository.

---

# Core Theme

The conceptual backbone of the book is:

Human Judgment

* AI Assistance
* Smart Workflows
  = **Leverage**

The book repeatedly demonstrates how remote professionals can use AI to multiply their effectiveness.

---

# Reader Journey

The manuscript is structured to guide readers through three stages:

1. **Understand the shift**
2. **Apply AI in real workflows**
3. **Build a reusable toolkit**

This progression is reflected in the three-part structure below.

---

When modifying the manuscript:
