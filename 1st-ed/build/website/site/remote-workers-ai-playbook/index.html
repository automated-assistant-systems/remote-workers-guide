    The Remote Worker’s AI Playbook (Cheat Sheet)

    1. Core Mindset

    • Experiment > Perfection

    • Human-first (empathy, creativity, strategy)

    • Stay curious (continuous learning)

    • Balance productivity + well-being

    2. Daily Workflow (The “AI Triage”)

    • Morning: Ask, “What can AI handle today?”

    • Summarize inbox + meetings with AI

    • Use AI to draft → refine manually

    • Alternate deep work + AI sprints

    3. Weekly Habits

    • Review AI-assisted outputs (time saved? quality?)

    • Share 1 experiment or learning publicly

    • Optimize automations (Zapier, Notion, Slack GPT)

    4. Monthly Habits

    • Audit skills → double down where AI assists, not replaces

    • Refresh brand (LinkedIn, portfolio, templates)

    • Try 1 new AI tool that supports your goals

    5. Essential Tool Stack (Quick Reference)

    Writing & Comms: ChatGPT, GrammarlyGO, Descript

    Research & Learning: Perplexity, Elicit, Duolingo Max

    Creativity & Branding: Canva AI, Midjourney, Runway

    Automation: Zapier, Make, Notion AI

    Collaboration: Otter.ai, Slack GPT, Fathom

    Well-being: Wysa, Calm, Sunsama

    (See Appendix for strengths/weaknesses + pricing notes)

    6. Guardrails

    Warning: Don’t trust AI blindly — always review

    Warning: Avoid tool overload — start with 2–3 core apps

    Warning: Keep human skills sharp (creativity, empathy)

    Warning: Don’t sacrifice health for hustle

    7. Action Plan: Your Next 30 Days

    Week 1: Build 3 branded templates with Canva AI

    Week 2: Automate 1 repetitive task with Zapier/Make

    Week 3: Repurpose 1 meeting/webinar into content via Descript

    Week 4: Publish 1 post about your AI workflow on LinkedIn

    8. The Remote Worker’s North Star

    Tip: AI is your co-pilot, not your replacement.

    Your edge = human judgment + AI acceleration.
